What is a Covenant?

A covenant is an extension of kinship by oath.

Through covenants, God makes us part of his family.

Adamic Covenant

God formed a covenant with Adam, making him the first Son of God:

  • Adam as the first Priest
  • Adam as the first Prophet
  • Adam as the Universal King and Bridegroom
